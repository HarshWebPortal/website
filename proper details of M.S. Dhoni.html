About His Cricket Career :-

He is an former International Cricketer and an former Captain of INDIA Team. He played his first International Cricket Match in 23 December 2004 (23/12/2004) against Sri Lanka at M.A. Chidambaram Stadium in (Chennai/India) and he played his last International Cricket Match in 9 July 2019 (09/072019) against New Zealand. He had scored 148 runs in 123 balls with 15 boundaries and 4 towering sixes in his fifth International Match in 5 April 2005 (05/04/2005) in Visakhapatnam aginst Pakistan .
